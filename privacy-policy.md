# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 04 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nithin Ayiluri ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with our software service that helps home-services businesses (such as plumbing, HVAC, and electrical contractors) communicate with their customers (the "Service"). If you have questions, contact us at nithinayiluri@gmail.com.

## Information we collect

We collect information needed to provide the Service, including:

- **Contact information** you provide or that a business provides about you — name, phone number, email address, and service address.
- **Service request details** — the nature of your request (e.g., a description of the problem), photos you choose to upload, preferred appointment times, and related notes.
- **Communications** — records of calls, text messages, and emails exchanged through the Service, and your consent to be contacted.
- **Payment information** — when you pay an invoice, payment is processed by our third-party payment processor; we do not store full card numbers.
- **Usage and device information** — basic technical data (such as IP address and browser type) needed to operate and secure the Service.

We collect this information when you call a business that uses the Service, submit a request through an intake form, reply to a text message, or otherwise interact with the Service.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- respond to your service request and communicate with you about it;
- schedule, perform, and follow up on services;
- send estimates, invoices, and process payments;
- send service-related text messages and emails (see "Text messaging" below);
- operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Service; and
- comply with legal obligations.

## Text messaging (SMS)

If you provide your mobile phone number, we may send you **service-related text messages** on behalf of the business you contacted. These include: automated replies when your call is missed, appointment confirmations and reminders, follow-up messages, review requests, and maintenance reminders related to your service or prior service with the business.

- **Consent:** You consent to receive these messages by contacting the business (for example, by calling it and receiving a reply, by replying to a text, or by submitting an intake form and checking a box agreeing to be contacted by text).
- **Frequency:** Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the business.
- **Rates:** Message and data rates may apply.
- **Opt-out:** You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. Reply **HELP** for help.
- **No sale or sharing of opt-in data:** **We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or your text-messaging opt-in/consent with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes.** Mobile opt-in data is used only to deliver the messages described above.

## How we share information

We share information only as needed to provide the Service:

- **With the business you contacted**, so it can respond to and perform your requested service.
- **With service providers** who process data on our behalf to operate the Service — for example, our telephony/SMS provider (to send messages) and our payment processor (to process payments). These providers may use the information only to perform services for us.
- **For legal reasons**, if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the Service.

**We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data with third parties for marketing.**

## Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, after which we delete or de-identify it.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. To make a request, contact us at nithinayiluri@gmail.com. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable law.

## Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a new effective date.
